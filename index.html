Our Vision

Magnetic Resonance Imaging is a game-changer among modern non-invasive imaging modalities and has been instrumental in advancing the global human health including early detection of diseases, precision medicine, cancer imaging, understanding brain function and mental health disorders etc. Unfortunately, the access to MRI has been restricted to developed countries and the upper-middle class to high-income groups due to the expensive operation of complex superconducting MRI magnet and the associated technological challenges. MagRes Innovatios has the vision to bring to market innovative solutions for low-cost cryogen-free mobile MRI systems which will ensure accessibility and affordability of MRI to the masses primarily in the developing countries.

In the aftermath of the Covid-19 pandemic, the world is moving towards decentralized healthcare systems and bring primary imaging care close to the patients. Our mission is to revolutionize primary healthcare in MR imaging by introducing mobile dedicated MRI systems and offer it as a service to GP surgeries and remote medical or research institutes.
